About Overtime Coffee

Overtime Coffee is a popular cafe in Ciamis that offers both indoor and outdoor seating, each with its own cozy atmosphere. Whether you prefer the warmth of the indoors or the fresh outdoor air, Overtime Coffee provides the perfect place to unwind.

The cafe is surrounded by lush greenery, offering natural air and a tranquil ambiance. Visitors can also enjoy live music, typically in the afternoon or evening, creating a lively yet relaxed environment.

With spacious areas and neutral color tones, Overtime Coffee is a favorite spot for Instagram-worthy photos. The cafe features contemporary photo spots both indoors and outdoors, making it a popular choice for those looking to capture memories.

At Overtime Coffee, we don't just offer great coffee. Our delicious and distinctive coffee flavors come at an affordable price. Additionally, visitors can enjoy various food offerings, including grilled donuts, fried ice cream, and dim sum.

For those looking to freshen up their hair, Overtime Coffee also houses a barbershop. Get your hair done while enjoying a cup of coffee or snack—it's a unique experience that sets us apart from other cafes.
